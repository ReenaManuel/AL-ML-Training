Observations :¶

Cluster 0 is characterised by lower credit limit , low # of credit cards, medium bank visit ,medium online visit , high calls

Cluster 1 is characterised by medium credit limit , medium # of credit cards, high bank visit , low online visit , medium calls

Cluster 2 is characterised by high credit limit , high # of credit cards, low bank visit , high online visit , low calls

Key Questions:¶

1) How many different segments of customers are there?

 Based on spending pattern 3 segment of 
 cluster 0--customers low spendin
 cluster 1--medium spending
 cluster 2--high spending

2) How are these segments different from each other?

 Segemnts are differnt based on there spending behaviour ,number of credit cards  and communications to bank .

3) What are your recommendations to the bank on how to better market to and service these customers?

 Cluster 0 -- Bank can provide new offer so customers in this segment use the credit card more often on purchases ,use  phone calls as means of marketing.
 Cluster 1 --May be a cash back will encourage these segement of customers to use credit card more ,marketing can be 
 done  when they visit bank.
 Cluster 2-- loyalty programs may help bank to keep these customers for long time ,means of marketing is thru online
